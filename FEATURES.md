# AI Marketing OS Features

## Version 0.1 (MVP)

### 1. Ad Generator
Generate complete Meta ads including:
- Hooks
- Primary Text
- Headlines
- CTA
- Image Prompt
- Video Prompt

### 2. Campaign Analyzer
Analyze marketing campaign metrics and provide optimization advice.

### 3. Content Generator
Generate social media posts, captions, reels ideas and content plans.

### 4. Prompt Generator
Generate high-quality AI prompts for marketing tasks.

### 5. Audience Finder
Suggest target audiences, interests and customer personas.

### 6. Creative Generator
Generate creative advertising ideas and concepts.
